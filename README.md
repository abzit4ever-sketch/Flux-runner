### Gameplay

![Gameplays](screenshots/gameplay.png)


A fast-paced hypercasual color-switching game. Roll, react, and shift your core's color on the fly to smash through matching barriers — one wrong move and it's game over. Simple to pick up, hard to put down, with coins to collect along the way and a satisfying rhythm of split-second decisions.
Current Status: Active Development 🚧
Flux Runner is playable now, with the core gameplay loop fully functional. A PvP Arena mode is currently in progress — this will let players go head-to-head in real time rather than just chasing solo high scores. It's an early build, so expect rough edges, and treat this release as a preview rather than a finished product.
